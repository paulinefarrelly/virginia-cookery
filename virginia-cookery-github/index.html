About us

Your host at Virginia Cookery is Finola O’Sullivan.

My ambition is to share cookery skills with you. Past students in my healthy eating cookery classes really enjoyed creating meals from everyday ingredients. I decided to go further and offer a variety of classes and courses suitable for every ability.

I am a County Cavan native and I invite you to come to our beautiful lakeland county. Enjoy the fresh air and drumlin views. Come along and try your hand at Irish cooking, and taste our terrific Cavan produce.

Arrange a class

We offer tailor-made cookery experiences for groups. These fun and informal events can be arranged for day or evening. The group cooks a meal together and all sit down to enjoy their creations. We usually include an extra dessert!

Sessions are three to four hours depending on your requirements. Time and date are flexible. Choose a menu from our upcoming classes or contact us to discuss variations.

Groups we can cater for: celebrations, community groups, social clubs, active retirement groups, college students, Men’s Shed, workplace team building, tourist groups, youth groups, and friends together just for fun.

Contact us to book

What to expect

  • We cater for all ages and skill levels.
  • Most classes can be arranged for morning or evening.
  • Guests usually work in pairs or together in your group.
  • Arrive ten to fifteen minutes before start time for welcome tea or coffee and a homemade snack or local cheese and crackers.
  • There is ample free parking on site.
  • We provide ingredients and aprons.
  • Wear comfortable shoes with toes and heels enclosed. Bring a lunchbox for leftovers.

Visiting Cavan

Are you looking for a fun experience during your holiday? Book a cookery class as part of your visit to County Cavan. We’re delighted to share Irish recipes and local produce with you in our hands-on cookery classes.

Classes are from three hours up to half a day depending on your itinerary. Contact us and let us know when you’re visiting the area and we will work to organise a class for you.

After your class, explore Ireland’s Hidden Heartlands: Virginia, Cavan County Museum, the Cavan Burren, Loughcrew Cairns, local restaurants and the Ramor Theatre.
